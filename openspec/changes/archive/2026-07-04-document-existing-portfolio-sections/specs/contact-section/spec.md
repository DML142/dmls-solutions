## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Animated black hole scene
The Contact section SHALL present a full 3D scene depicting an animated black
hole as its background, positioned as the final section in the scroll flow.

#### Scenario: Scrolling to the end of the page
- **WHEN** the user scrolls to the Contact section
- **THEN** an animated 3D black hole scene fills the section's background

### Requirement: Contact information overlay
The Contact section SHALL overlay minimal text with the site owner's contact
information and links on top of the black hole scene.

#### Scenario: Contact section is visible
- **WHEN** the Contact section is in view
- **THEN** contact information and links are legible over the animated
  background
